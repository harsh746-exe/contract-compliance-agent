# Q2 2025 Quarterly Business Review

## Executive Summary

Q2 was dominated by clinic expansion onboarding and security hygiene work. Service levels remained within target,
though analyst workload increased during site cutovers.

## Highlights

- eight acquired sites onboarded
- average SLA attainment: 97.1 percent
- 94 new users provisioned
- vulnerability backlog reduced from 41 to 19 high-risk items

## Risks

- backup log shipping for the tier-1 EHR support database was still running hourly
- patch scheduling windows were missed at two locations during acquisition cutover

## Actions

- move tier-1 systems to 15-minute log shipping in Q3
- formalize acquisition cutover checklist
