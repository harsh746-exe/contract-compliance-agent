# Northstar IT Services 2025 Mock Company System

This folder is a realistic mock document corpus for a fictional managed IT services provider.
It is intended to support:

- compliance review demos
- proposal drafting demos
- retrieval and evidence-grounding tests
- stakeholder dashboard walkthroughs
- end-to-end document orchestration experiments

## Company Summary

Northstar IT Services is a mid-sized managed services company supporting the fictional client
`Ridgeway Care Network`, a regional healthcare and administrative services organization.

Operating scope for 2025:

- service desk and endpoint support
- cloud operations and infrastructure monitoring
- security operations coordination
- vulnerability remediation coordination
- disaster recovery planning and testing
- executive governance reporting
- training, onboarding, and knowledge transfer

## Corpus Layout

- `00_company/` company background and operating profile
- `01_contracts/` MSA, SOW, and change orders
- `02_procurement/` RFP and vendor-response style material
- `03_governance/` quarterly business reviews
- `04_operations/` monthly service reports for January through December 2025
- `05_security/` controls, assessments, and penetration-test summary
- `06_resilience/` disaster recovery exercise material
- `07_people/` staffing and training records
- `08_finance/` invoice and billing summary
- `09_changes/` yearly change calendar
- `10_incidents/` major incident register

## Notes

- All organizations, names, metrics, and events in this folder are fictional.
- Dates and operational trends are designed to feel internally consistent across the year.
- Some documents intentionally show partial gaps or operational risk so the corpus is useful for compliance reasoning.
