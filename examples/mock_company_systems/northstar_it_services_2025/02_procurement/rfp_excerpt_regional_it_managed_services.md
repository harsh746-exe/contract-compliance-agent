# RFP Excerpt: Regional IT Managed Services

Issue date: November 20, 2024

## Required Capabilities

- weekly incident reporting and monthly executive governance review
- AES-256 encryption for hosted client data at rest
- TLS 1.2 or higher for all external communications
- weekday support coverage from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM Central Time
- 15-minute acknowledgement for severity 1 incidents
- documented disaster recovery with 4-hour RTO and 15-minute RPO
- onboarding and administrator training support
- documented change management and quarterly resilience testing

## Evaluation Criteria

- operational maturity of the service desk
- quality of reporting and governance
- strength of security controls
- evidence of continuity testing
- ability to support multi-site healthcare-adjacent operations
