# Vendor Response Executive Summary

Northstar IT Services proposes a fully managed support and operations model for Ridgeway Care Network.

## Commitments

- weekly incident digests and monthly executive governance packages
- weekday live support from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM CT
- severity 1 acknowledgement in 15 minutes or less
- AES-256 encryption for hosted storage and TLS 1.2+ for external traffic
- quarterly disaster recovery exercises with reporting
- administrator enablement and user quick-start materials during rollout phases

## Delivery Position

Northstar brings a regional service desk, healthcare-operating experience, cloud administration coverage,
and an established governance cadence. The primary improvement area identified during transition planning
was recovery point performance for tier-1 systems, which Northstar committed to improve through staged
replication enhancements during the 2025 operating year.
