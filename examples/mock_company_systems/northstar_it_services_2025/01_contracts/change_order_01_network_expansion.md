# Change Order 01: Network Expansion

Effective date: April 15, 2025

## Reason

Ridgeway acquired eight outpatient locations during Q2 2025 and required those sites to be added to the managed network and service desk footprint.

## Scope Added

- remote monitoring for eight branch firewalls
- onboarding of 126 new endpoint devices
- support for 94 new users
- site cutover planning and post-cutover hypercare

## Delivery Impact

- field services staffing increased by one contractor through July 2025
- onboarding ticket volume expected to rise by 18 percent during Q2
- knowledge articles for clinic onboarding updated by May 1, 2025
