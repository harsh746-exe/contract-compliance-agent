# Master Services Agreement Excerpt

Effective date: January 1, 2025  
Parties: Ridgeway Care Network and Northstar IT Services

## Key Service Obligations

1. Northstar shall deliver a weekly operational incident summary and a monthly executive governance report.
2. Customer data at rest shall be encrypted using AES-256 or stronger controls.
3. External data transmissions shall use TLS 1.2 or higher.
4. Weekday support coverage shall be maintained from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM Central Time.
5. Severity 1 incidents shall be acknowledged within 15 minutes.
6. Disaster recovery capabilities shall support a 4-hour recovery time objective.
7. Disaster recovery capabilities shall support a 15-minute recovery point objective.
8. Administrator training and end-user quick reference material shall be provided within 30 days of major rollout milestones.

## Governance

- Monthly governance reviews shall include SLA attainment, open risks, staffing changes, security issues, and upcoming milestones.
- Material gaps against security, continuity, or service level obligations shall be escalated for client review within one business day.
