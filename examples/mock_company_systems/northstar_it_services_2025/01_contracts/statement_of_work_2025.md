# 2025 Statement of Work

## Services Included

- service desk intake and triage
- endpoint support and device lifecycle coordination
- infrastructure monitoring and systems administration
- monthly patch governance
- incident and problem management
- quarterly disaster recovery testing
- monthly executive reporting
- onboarding, administrator training, and knowledge artifacts

## Delivery Measures

- service desk coverage: Monday through Friday, 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM CT
- severity 1 acknowledgement: 15 minutes
- severity 2 acknowledgement: 30 minutes
- executive reporting cadence: monthly, with weekly incident rollups
- recovery time objective: 4 hours
- recovery point objective: 15 minutes for tier-1 hosted systems

## Exclusions

- custom application development
- unmanaged biomedical devices
- after-hours desk support for standard requests
- hardware procurement outside approved refresh plan
