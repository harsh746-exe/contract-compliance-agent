# Northstar IT Services

## Overview

Northstar IT Services is a fictional managed services provider specializing in mid-market healthcare,
regional professional services, and distributed office operations.

For calendar year 2025, Northstar supported `Ridgeway Care Network` under a managed services engagement
covering 42 sites across Illinois, Indiana, and Wisconsin.

## Delivery Footprint

- 1,850 supported end users
- 42 branch and clinic locations
- 620 managed servers and cloud workloads
- Microsoft 365, Azure, VMware, Palo Alto, and SentinelOne environment support
- weekday managed support window from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM Central Time
- on-call major incident response for after-hours severity escalation

## Operating Teams

- service desk: 12 analysts
- infrastructure operations: 6 engineers
- security and compliance: 4 analysts
- client success and governance: 3 managers
- field services: 5 technicians

## 2025 Focus Areas

- raise executive reporting quality and consistency
- improve recovery point performance from hourly replication to 15-minute log shipping
- formalize endpoint patching and vulnerability follow-through
- support expansion into eight newly acquired outpatient sites
- reduce repeat password-reset and onboarding tickets through training and self-service
