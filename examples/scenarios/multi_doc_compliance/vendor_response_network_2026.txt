1. DELIVERY MODEL
We will issue a weekly incident digest and a monthly executive governance package covering SLA attainment, open risks, staffing updates, subcontractor performance, and the next implementation milestones.

2. SECURITY APPROACH
Our managed platform runs in a FedRAMP Moderate environment, and we maintain a control crosswalk mapped to NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 families for the deployed services.

3. ACCESS CONTROLS
We enforce multifactor authentication for privileged accounts and support PIV-enabled single sign-on for government administrators accessing the management portal.

4. SERVICE LEVELS
We commit to 99.95 percent monthly availability and maintain an operations bridge to coordinate restoration activities.

5. INCIDENT MANAGEMENT
Our service desk acknowledges Severity 1 incidents within 15 minutes and targets restoration within 4 hours for platform outages.

6. SECURITY MONITORING
All platform and device logs are forwarded to the enterprise SIEM and are retained online for 365 days for search and investigation.

7. VULNERABILITY REMEDIATION
We perform weekly authenticated scans and close critical vulnerabilities within 10 calendar days unless the government approves a documented exception.

8. STAFFING PLAN
We will provide one onsite program manager and three cleared network engineers, with a fourth engineer available remotely for surge support.

9. TRANSITION EXECUTION
We will deliver a 30-day transition plan that includes milestone owners, rehearsal checkpoints, and rollback procedures for the cutover weekend.

10. CMDB DISCIPLINE
Production changes are recorded in the asset inventory within 2 business days through our automated configuration workflow.

11. RESILIENCE
We maintain a disaster recovery capability with a tested 2-hour recovery target and conduct an annual failover exercise with the customer.

12. ENABLEMENT
We will deliver two administrator workshops and four user familiarization sessions during the first 45 days of performance.

13. CONTRACT COMPLIANCE
Our program office follows FAR 52.204-21 safeguarding requirements and will administer commercial services consistent with FAR 52.212-4 terms.

14. SMALL BUSINESS PLAN
We provide a monthly subcontracting report identifying small business participation, variances, and corrective actions.

15. SOFTWARE SUPPLY CHAIN
We will provide an SBOM for the core monitoring platform but not for third-party utility tools unless specifically requested after award.
