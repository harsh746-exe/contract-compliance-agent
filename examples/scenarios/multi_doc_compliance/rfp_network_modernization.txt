1. PROGRAM REPORTING
The contractor shall deliver a weekly operational incident summary and a monthly executive governance report covering service levels, open risks, staffing changes, subcontractor updates, and upcoming milestones.

2. FEDRAMP BOUNDARY
The contractor must operate the managed platform within a FedRAMP Moderate authorized boundary and maintain a current System Security Plan aligned to NIST SP 800-53 Rev. 5 controls.

3. IDENTITY MANAGEMENT
The contractor shall enforce multifactor authentication for all privileged users and shall support PIV or CAC authentication for government administrators.

4. AVAILABILITY
The contractor shall maintain 99.95 percent monthly service availability for the production network management platform.

5. INCIDENT RESPONSE
The contractor shall acknowledge Severity 1 incidents within 15 minutes and restore service within 4 hours unless a written waiver is approved by the COR.

6. LOG RETENTION
The contractor must forward security logs to the enterprise SIEM and retain searchable logs for at least 365 calendar days.

7. VULNERABILITY MANAGEMENT
The contractor shall perform weekly authenticated vulnerability scans and remediate critical findings within 15 calendar days.

8. STAFFING
The contractor shall provide one onsite program manager and four cleared network engineers dedicated to the task order.

9. TRANSITION
The contractor shall submit a 30-day transition plan with a cutover rehearsal schedule, rollback steps, and named owners for each transition milestone.

10. CONFIGURATION MANAGEMENT
The contractor shall update the configuration management database and authoritative asset inventory within 2 business days of any production change.

11. DISASTER RECOVERY
The contractor shall maintain a tested disaster recovery capability that restores core network management functions within 2 hours and shall conduct one failover exercise each year.

12. TRAINING
The contractor shall deliver two administrator training workshops and four end-user familiarization sessions during the first 60 days of performance.

13. FAR SAFEGUARDING
The contractor must comply with FAR 52.204-21 Basic Safeguarding requirements and the commercial terms in FAR 52.212-4.

14. SUBCONTRACTING REPORTS
The contractor shall provide a monthly subcontracting utilization report identifying small business participation and any variances from plan.

15. SOFTWARE TRANSPARENCY
The contractor shall provide a software bill of materials for all newly deployed management tools before production release.
