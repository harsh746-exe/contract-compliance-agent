1. SECURITY NOTIFICATION
The contractor shall provide timely notice of material cybersecurity events when appropriate for the situation.

2. IDENTITY SUPPORT
The solution must support common federal identity methods where feasible for administrative users.

3. STAFFING LEVELS
The contractor shall maintain sufficient staffing to avoid undue delays during business-critical incidents.

4. REPORTING CONTENT
Monthly reports shall include relevant performance indicators acceptable to the COR.

5. ENCRYPTION
The contractor must use industry-standard encryption or equivalent protections as applicable to the data involved.
