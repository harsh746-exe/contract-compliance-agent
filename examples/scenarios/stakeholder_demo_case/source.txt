1. EXECUTIVE REPORTING
The contractor shall deliver a weekly incident summary and a monthly executive governance report that includes service levels, open risks, staffing changes, and upcoming milestones.

2. DATA SECURITY
The contractor must encrypt customer data at rest using AES-256 and encrypt all external data transmissions using TLS 1.2 or higher.

3. SERVICE DESK COVERAGE
The contractor shall provide weekday support coverage from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM Central Time and acknowledge Severity 1 incidents within 15 minutes.

4. BUSINESS CONTINUITY
The contractor shall maintain a disaster recovery posture with a recovery time objective of 4 hours and a recovery point objective of 15 minutes.

5. KNOWLEDGE TRANSFER
The contractor shall provide administrator training and publish end-user quick reference guides within 30 calendar days of implementation kickoff.
