1. REPORTING APPROACH
We will issue a weekly incident digest and a monthly executive governance package that covers SLA attainment, open delivery risks, staffing updates, and the next implementation milestones.

2. SECURITY CONTROLS
Our platform encrypts customer data at rest with AES-256 and protects all external traffic with TLS 1.2 or higher. We also complete an annual third-party penetration test.

3. SUPPORT MODEL
Our managed services team provides live weekday support from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM Central Time. Severity 1 incidents are acknowledged within 15 minutes and escalated immediately to the on-call response lead.

4. RESILIENCE MODEL
Our disaster recovery design supports a 4-hour recovery time objective. Database snapshots are replicated hourly across regions and retained for rapid restoration.

5. ENABLEMENT PLAN
We deliver administrator training workshops and publish end-user quick reference guides within the first 30 calendar days after project kickoff.
