1. EXECUTIVE APPROACH
The contractor shall provide an executive summary that explains how the proposed cyber operations model reduces incident response time and improves audit readiness.

2. TRANSITION PLAN
The contractor shall provide a 45-day transition plan with staffing, knowledge transfer activities, risk controls, and measurable entry and exit criteria.

3. STAFFING MATRIX
The contractor shall identify a program manager, a security operations lead, a threat hunting lead, and a quality manager in the proposal response.

4. REPORTING
The contractor shall provide a monthly cyber operations report covering incidents, vulnerabilities, threat hunting outcomes, and remediation status.

5. AUTOMATION
The contractor shall describe the automation used for log aggregation, case management, and evidence collection for audits.

6. PAST PERFORMANCE
The contractor shall cite at least two prior efforts that demonstrate experience with federal SOC modernization or continuous monitoring programs.
